    Artist, Producer, Storyteller

    BIO

    PUTRID is a musician, artist, and storyteller known for pushing the boundaries of sound and narrative. With a background in Music Production from the Los Angeles Film School and a passion for experimental art forms, they create work that bridges the raw intensity of noise and music with the emotional depth of storytelling.

    Drawing on a wide range of influences, PUTRID's musical journey began with the immersive sounds of rock and metal bands like Tool, Pink Floyd, and Slipknot. This foundation evolved into a love for heavier genres such as deathcore and slamcore, inspired by bands like Cannibal Corpse and Infant Annihilator. The discovery of Vocaloid artists like Kikuo and Utsu-P opened the door to electronic music, which soon expanded to the chaotic, sample-driven realms of breakcore and harsh noise. Today, PUTRID blends these diverse influences into a unique fusion of distortion, rhythm, and emotion.

    Performing under the evolving artist name PUTRID, they are dedicated to creating raw, unfiltered soundscapes that resonate deeply with listeners seeking something outside the mainstream. With a fearless approach to both music and narrative, PUTRID invites audiences to step into a world where chaos becomes art.
